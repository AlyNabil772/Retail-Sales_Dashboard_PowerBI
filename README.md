# Retail-Sales_Dashboard_PowerBI
## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze retail sales data to evaluate overall business performance, identify sales trends, and understand customer purchasing behavior.
The analysis aims to support data-driven decisions related to product strategy, regional performance, and sales optimization.

## Dataset Used
- Sales transaction data including products, categories, customers, geography, and time dimensions.
- Questions (KPIs)
- What is the total sales revenue and total profit?
- How many units were sold?
- What is the overall profit margin?
- Which product categories and sub-categories perform best?
- How does sales performance vary by state and region?
- How do sales trends change over time (monthly)?
- What are the key customer demographics (age & gender)?
- Which regions and products drive the highest revenue?

## Process
- Verified data quality and handled missing or inconsistent values.
- Performed data cleaning and transformation.
- Built a data model with proper relationships.
- Created DAX measures for KPIs and calculations.
- Designed an interactive Power BI dashboard focused on business insights.
- Applied best practices in dashboard layout and visual hierarchy.

## Dashboard
- The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of retail sales performance, including:
- Executive-level KPIs (Total Sales, Cost, Profit, Quantity).
- Sales distribution by product category and sub-category.
- Sales performance by state and region.
- Monthly sales trends.
- Customer insights by age group and gender.
- Identification of top-performing products and locations.

## Project Insights
- The business generates strong total revenue with a healthy profit margin.
- A small number of product categories contribute the majority of total sales.
- Certain sub-categories consistently outperform others and act as key revenue drivers.
- Sales are concentrated in a few high-performing states, while other regions show moderate performance.
- Monthly trends indicate seasonal patterns that affect sales volume.
- The majority of customers fall within the 25â€“45 age range, with relatively balanced gender distribution.

## Final Conclusion
- Focusing on top-performing categories and sub-categories can maximize revenue.
- High-performing regions should be prioritized with targeted marketing and premium offerings.
- Seasonal sales patterns can be leveraged to optimize inventory and promotions.
- Customer demographic insights can support more effective marketing strategies.
- This dashboard demonstrates how raw sales data can be transformed into actionable business insights to support strategic and operational decision-making.
